# Article Summary: Understanding Customer Behavior

## Key Takeaways from Medium Analysis

This project is based on the insights from my Medium article "Understanding Customer Behavior and Maximizing Profitability."

### Core Findings:
1. **Pareto Principle Confirmation:** Approximately 80% of revenue came from 20% of customers (High-Value Loyalists)
2. **Four Distinct Segments:** Identified through RFM analysis, each requiring different marketing approaches
3. **Actionable Strategies:** Developed specific campaigns for each segment to improve retention and CLV

### Methodology:
- **Tool:** Excel for data analysis and segmentation
- **Framework:** RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis
- **Outcome:** Strategic marketing framework for targeted customer engagement
